Returns Policy

Returning an item is handled case-by-case to ensure customers receive the right resolution based on warranty and product condition.

Eligibility

  • Items should be returned in good condition and with proof of purchase.
  • Warrantable issues are usually handled under the warranty process.
  • Return eligibility depends on the product category and the reason for return.

Common reasons

  • Incorrect item delivered (must be reported promptly)
  • Damaged item on arrival
  • Defective product reported within the warranty period

How to request a return

Contact Customer Service with your order details. Our team will confirm eligibility and provide next steps (pickup instructions or service assessment).
