Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about ordering, delivery, warranties and returns.
How do I place an order?

Browse products, choose the item, and add it to your cart. Complete checkout using the contact/payment details provided.

Do you deliver nationwide in Ghana?

Yes. We deliver across Ghana with trusted handling. Delivery timelines depend on your location and product availability.

What payment options do you offer?

We offer flexible payment plans, including the option to pay small when it reaches. Details appear during checkout.

Is there warranty on products?

Yes. Most products include manufacturer warranty. Coverage duration depends on the product brand/model and will be confirmed with your receipt.

How do I track my order?

Use the Track My Order page. Sign in to view the order timeline (demo data is stored under your account).

How do returns work?

Return eligibility depends on the product condition and warranty policy. Contact us with your order details to confirm next steps.

How can I contact Customer Service?

You can reach us by phone, email, or WhatsApp using the information in the footer on any page.
